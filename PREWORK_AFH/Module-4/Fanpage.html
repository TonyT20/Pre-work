ABBA

Abba is a pop band that was formed in Stockholm Sweden in 1972. The pop band consisted four members, two vocalist and two musician. The bandmates were also married couples. The pop group consist of two group of couples. One pair of couple was Fältskog and Ulvaeu and the other was Lyngstad and Andersson. They're marriage was good but throughout the years of fame both pairs grew apart and eventually both divorced. Hence making one of their last song about their relationship "Winner takes it all." Ending their pop group in 1982, but recently in 2018 trying to make a comeback.
